##################
# v0.1.0 - Alpha #
##################

Initial Dust Dynasty: Diggers Unite implementation

- Registry-driven clicker game architecture (OreDefinition, UpgradeDefinition,
  WorkerDefinition, PrestigeDefinition, ClickerTerrainInstruction, DepthMilestone)
- ClickerConfig resource for all tuning values (no hardcoded numbers)
- ClickerDataManager autoload: loads all registries from folder paths configured in editor
- ClickerGameState autoload: tap power, workers DPS, prestige multiplier, all derived stats
- ClickerSaveManager: ConfigFile persistence to user://, auto-save every 30s,
  offline earnings calculated from last_online timestamp
- 6 ore types: dirt, stone, clay, coal, iron, gold
- 4 terrain layers with FastNoiseLite ore distributions (shallow/mid/deep/abyss)
- 6 upgrades: tap power, tap speed, crit chance, crit multiplier, ore value, offline rate
- 4 auto workers: apprentice digger, miner, driller, blaster
- 4 prestige bonuses: dust multiplier, worker efficiency, offline bonus, tap power bonus
- DiggingView: procedural grid, tap detection, row-clear depth advance, floating labels
- ClickerHUD: coin/depth/dust/DPS display, bottom-bar panel toggles
- ShopPanel, WorkersPanel, PrestigePanel, SettingsPanel with scrollable registry rows
- FloatingLabel scene for tap coin feedback
- Project configured for Godot 4.2 GL Compatibility (web export ready)
